About Infinity Clicker

Infinity clicker is an incremental game, which pretty much means that you have a number that you want to increase. To make increasing it easier, you buy upgrades that set you back from your goal, but improve the speed at which you get to it.

Number

The number is your main currency in this game.
Since this game is abstract, there are no coins or physical things, there's just a number. It has no symbol so if you see for example 'Cost: 10' then it cost 10 of your number.

NPC

NPC or Numbers Per Click is a measure of how much your number increases every click.

NPS

NPS stands for Numbers Per Second and it measures how much your number increases every second.

Dimensions

Dimensions, written as '[type][dim]' are a fundimental part of Infinity Clicker.
They are quite simple: The higher dimension generates the lower dimension.
For example:
A third NPC dimension (NPC3) will generate second NPC dimensions (NPC2)

Achivements

Achivements are small optional tasks that you can do for a small boost.
Psst! There are some secret achivements...

Upgrades

Upgrades make you progress faster. They can be essential things such as autobuyers or optional things like boosts

Challenges

Challenges are hard tasks that can an essential upgrade or give you a large boost.
